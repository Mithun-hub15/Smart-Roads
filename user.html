User Emergency Guidelines:
  • Stay calm. Take a deep breath and analyze the situation.
  • Move to a safe location if you feel threatened or in danger.
  • Use the emergency numbers above to call for help immediately if required.
  • Share your real-time location with authorities if possible.
  • Assist others near you only if it is safe for you to do so.
  • Do not block or crowd emergency access routes.
  • Avoid spreading panic or unverified information on social media.
  • Follow instructions from police, doctors, or emergency staff.
  • Document details (photos, notes) only if safe and not interfering with rescue.
  • Report the incident accurately and cooperate fully with responders.
